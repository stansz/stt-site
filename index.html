Welcome to Speech to Text 👋

Choose an AI model size, download it, and start transcribing — entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

  • Tiny (40MB) — Fastest, good for quick notes Default
  • Base (75MB) — Balanced speed and accuracy
  • Small (250MB) — Most accurate, slower
  • Record mode — Click mic, speak, click again to stop. Most reliable.
  • Real-time mode — Streams audio in chunks, transcribes live. Experimental.
  • Upload mode — Upload an audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.) for transcription.
